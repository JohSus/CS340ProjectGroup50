Busy Beaver Burgers

Orders

Orders are made by customers, and they are typically transactions, meaning they are recorded and generally not changed. When recorded, they contain the date and time of the transaction, the customer that ordered the food, the quantity of each dish ordered, and the cumulative price of the food (not shown, because it is a calculation of individual food items).

Customers

The customers that order food or will prospectively order food from Busy Beaver Burgers. We want to record customer details to provide a more customized experience (especially with regards to allergies). Additionally, we want to keep track of customers who leave poor reviews to follow up with them afterwards.

Dishes

These are the dishes on the menu, of which there are currently 7. Each dish has a name, price, order quantity (the number of the same dish that is ordered in one order). Each dish also can be associated with one or more dietary restrictions.

Orders has Dishes

This is intersection page for Orders and Dishes. A specific order can have multiple dishes and each dish in an order has its own record.

Ratings

Ratings are between 1 and 5, and they give the restaurant an idea of which dishes are popular at the moment.

Dietary Restrictions

The dietary restrictions table is a category table that lists categories such as “vegan”, “gluten-free”, etc., and it describes foods on the menu, as well as customers should they choose to list their dietary preferences.

Customer Dietary Restrictions

This is the intersection page for Customers and Dietary Restrictions. A customer can have multiple dietary restrictions, each of which are in seperate records.
